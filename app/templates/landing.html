About Us

The Priority innovation Development Unit (PID) formerly the Teacher Centre Project Team is a support function that serves the College of Education for purposes for furthering student throughput and bridging the digital divide for rural communities in the interest of expanding Open Distance Electronic Learning. Our mandate is to improve student support, increase academic participation and teacher development through innovative physical and digital solutions such as the e-Learning Labs @ Teacher Centres and Tippy Tube.
